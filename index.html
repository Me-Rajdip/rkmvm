Dr. Goutam Paul

Computer Scientist, Cryptographer & Educator

Dr. Goutam Paul completed his B.E. from IIEST Shibpur, MS from SUNY Albany, USA, and PhD from Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) Kolkata. Formerly a faculty at Jadavpur University, he has been serving at ISI Kolkata for over 12 years. His research spans classical cryptography and quantum computation, with significant contributions to both fields.

Research Areas

Classical Cryptography, Quantum Computation & Communication, Cybersecurity, Algorithm Design.

Awards

Alexander von Humboldt Award (Germany), Young Scientist Award (NASI), Multiple Best Paper Awards.

Talks & Mentorship

150+ Technical Lectures, 100+ Motivational Speeches, 2 TEDx talks. Mentored 100+ students and researchers.

Interests

World literature, poetry (Bengali), photography, nature, cricket, and Vedic spiritual science.

Since 2024, Dr. Paul is also serving as the Regional Coordinator of the Mathematical Olympiad for West Bengal Region, under National Board of Higher Mathematics (NBHM), India. He has supervised 6 Post-Docs, 16 Ph.D. Theses, 24 Master's Theses, and close to 100 Bachelor's Projects.
